Our Story

Our Story

Brothers Taquizas is a family-owned Mexican food truck born from the dream of David Herrera and his wife to bring the authentic flavors of home to South Florida. With deep roots in Guatemala, we grew up surrounded by the vibrant traditions of taquizas — those joyful gatherings where fresh handmade tortillas meet sizzling meats, bold salsas, and endless toppings for the perfect taco party.

Now parked at our prime spot on 3707 Broadway in Riviera Beach, we're proud to share that same taste of Guatemala and Mexico with our community — using only the freshest ingredients, time-honored recipes, and a whole lot of love. Whether you're craving al pastor tacos or planning your next event, we're here to make every bite feel like family.

Welcome to Brothers Taquizas — where every taquiza is a celebration.

FAQ

Mexican Food Near Me — Frequently Asked

Are you a taco truck near me in Riviera Beach?
Yes — Brothers Taquizas is parked daily at 3707 Broadway, Riviera Beach, FL 33404. We're a family-owned Mexican food truck serving fresh tacos, taquizas, burritos and more from 10 AM seven days a week.
Do you offer catering for events?
Yes — we offer pickup catering for weddings, corporate events, birthdays, and festivals. Order ahead and pick up fresh, hot trays at our Riviera Beach truck at 3707 Broadway. Build-your-own taco trays, party platters, and vegan/gluten-free options available. Call 561-201-5724 for a custom quote.
What's the best authentic Mexican food near me at Brothers Taquizas?
Our most-ordered dishes are the Birria Taco (slow-braised beef with consomé), Al Pastor Tacos (marinated pork with fresh pineapple), Fajitas Supreme, and The Cheesiest Quesadilla. Every tortilla is handmade and every salsa is made fresh daily.
What makes Brothers Taquizas authentic?
We're family-owned with deep Guatemalan roots, bringing traditional taquiza recipes to South Florida. Handmade tortillas, slow-braised meats, fresh-pressed salsas, and recipes passed down through generations — no shortcuts, no pre-made anything.
When is the food truck open?
Monday through Thursday 10 AM – 9 PM, Friday and Saturday 10 AM – 10 PM, and Sunday 12 PM – 10 PM. Follow us on Instagram @brotherstaquizas for real-time location updates and daily specials.
